# Harpenden.AI

A vision document (as a website) to present to Harpenden Town Council, showing what a town could look like if it embraced AI literacy at community scale. Developed by Hugo Pickford-Wardle under the AI Optimist brand.

## Working Here

- This is a **vision / pitch document delivered as a website**, not a live service. The audience is the Harpenden Town Council; the goal is for them to see a fully-formed opportunity with low effort on their side, so conversations skip "should we?" and go straight to "how do we help?".
- The site also functions as a sales surface for AI Night School, Sherpas AI, AI Optimist Strategy, AI Transformation Accelerator, Imaginarium, Problemarium, Chooser, and diagnostics.
- This is chargeable work - it monetises while also advancing Hugo's systemic-impact mission.
- Design inspiration: `www.startupsherpas.co.uk` and `www.ainightschool.org`. Use AI Optimist branding. Include local Harpenden imagery.
- Always attribute: "Developed by Hugo Pickford-Wardle - AI Optimist".

## Stack Preferences

- TypeScript over Python (per workspace CLAUDE.md).
- Use `bun` for JS/TS.
- For the site itself, default to Next.js + Tailwind unless we agree otherwise in PLAN.md.
- Markdown over HTML for content drafts - the site should build from structured content sources.

## Key Documents

- `BRIEF.md` - original brief from Hugo, tidied into structured form.
- `PLAN.md` - proposed site IA, phases, open questions.
- `research/` - demographics, council engagement data, population analysis.
- `content/` - section-by-section draft copy.
- `design/` - design system notes, imagery sources, brand references.
- `directory/` - content for the interventions directory page.
- `prototypes/` - worked examples (e.g. `/harpenden-traffic`) referenced from the site.
- `site/` - the Next.js application.

## Voice

- Optimistic, grounded, specific. Avoid AI hype clichés.
- Talk about people first (residents, businesses, teens, retirees, council), not technology.
- Frame risk honestly (London knowledge-economy displacement) without fear-mongering.
