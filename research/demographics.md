# Harpenden demographics - public sources

Every figure in the live site is drawn from the sources below. This file
is the research ledger so any number can be traced to its source.

## Population

- **31,128** - Harpenden civil parish, ONS Census 2021.
- **31,337** - ONS mid-2024 estimate.
- **Growth:** 28,030 (2001) → 29,835 (2011) → 30,966 / 31,128 (2021).
- Source: [CityPopulation - Harpenden](https://www.citypopulation.de/en/uk/eastofengland/hertfordshire/E63004326__harpenden/) (drawing on ONS), [Wikipedia - Harpenden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harpenden).

## Age split (mid-2024 estimate)

- Under 18: **8,440 (26.9%)**
- Working age 18–64: **17,267 (55.1%)**
- 65+: **5,630 (18.0%)**
- 40–59 cohort (the core at-risk knowledge-worker bulge): **9,839 (~31% of total)**
- Source: CityPopulation / ONS mid-year estimates.

## Occupation mix (ONS Census 2021)

- Harpenden North ward: 32.0% professional + 16.6% managerial/senior-official ≈ **48.6% combined**.
- Harpenden South ward: 29.8% + 19.1% ≈ **48.9% combined**.
- National comparators: 20.2% professional, 13.1% higher managerial NS-SeC.
- **St Albans District ranks 4th in England** for proportion of managers, senior officials and professionals.
- Sources: [LocalStats - Harpenden North](http://harpenden-north.localstats.co.uk/census-demographics/england/east-of-england/st-albans/harpenden-north), [Harpenden South](http://harpenden-south.localstats.co.uk/census-demographics/england/east-of-england/st-albans/harpenden-south), [ONS Industry &amp; Occupation 2021](https://www.ons.gov.uk/employmentandlabourmarket/peopleinwork/employmentandemployeetypes/bulletins/industryandoccupationenglandandwales/census2021), [St Albans C&amp;DC Census 2021 Overview](https://www.stalbans.gov.uk/sites/default/files/attachments/St%20Albans%20City%20and%20District%20Census%202021%20Data%20Overview.pdf).

## Commuting

- Thameslink, direct to St Pancras in **26–37 min**, up to 7 trains/hour at peak.
- Annual season ticket ~**£2,960**.
- Harpenden station: **2,644,230** entries/exits in 2023/24, ranked **194 of 2,581** in Great Britain.
- COVID-era Census 2021 travel-to-work data is unrepresentative (60.8% of Harpenden Common residents recorded "working from home" on Census Day).
- Sources: [Thameslink](https://www.thameslinkrailway.com/travel-inspiration/trains-to-harpenden), [CommuterGuide](https://www.commuterguide.co.uk/counties/hertfordshire/harpenden), [RailwayData - HPD](https://www.railwaydata.co.uk/stations/overview/?TLC=HPD), [ONS Travel to Work 2021](https://www.ons.gov.uk/employmentandlabourmarket/peopleinwork/employmentandemployeetypes/bulletins/traveltoworkenglandandwales/census2021).

## Income &amp; deprivation

- Hitchin &amp; Harpenden constituency ranked **10th** in England for average income tax paid (HMRC).
- Savills: Harpenden **7th** most valuable commuter area.
- IMD 2019: Harpenden North LSOA ranked **32,612 / 32,844** - **top 1% least deprived** in England.
- St Albans District: over 50% of LSOAs in the least-deprived national quintile.
- Sources: [Wikipedia - Harpenden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Harpenden), [UKLocalArea - Harpenden North](https://www.uklocalarea.com/index.php?lsoa=E01023694&amp;q=Harpenden+North&amp;wc=26UGGD), [IoD 2019 Statistical Release](https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/media/5d8e26f6ed915d5570c6cc55/IoD2019_Statistical_Release.pdf).

## Education

- **60.5%** of working-age Harpenden residents hold Level 4+ qualifications (degree or above) - vs **29.8%** medium-built-up-area peer-group average.
- Source: [ONS Towns &amp; Cities 2021](https://www.ons.gov.uk/peoplepopulationandcommunity/housing/articles/townsandcitiescharacteristicsofbuiltupareasenglandandwales/census2021).

## Schools

Harpenden Secondary Schools Trust - four state secondaries linked with
University of Hertfordshire and Rothamsted Research:

- Roundwood Park School (~1,300 pupils, non-selective academy)
- Sir John Lawes School (Ofsted Outstanding)
- St George’s School (joined trust 2013)
- Katherine Warington School (joined 2019)

Source: [Roundwood Park - Trust page](https://roundwoodpark.co.uk/harpenden-secondary-schools-trust/).

## Key community anchors

- **Rothamsted Research** - world’s oldest agricultural research station (founded 1843); 330-hectare campus; 500+ staff, 300 scientists. [rothamsted.ac.uk](https://www.rothamsted.ac.uk/).
- **Harpenden railway station** - Thameslink on Midland Main Line.
- **Harpenden Common** - central open space.
- **St Nicholas Church / Parish** - [harpenden.parish.uk](https://harpenden.parish.uk/).
- **Harpenden Leisure Centre**.
- **We Are Harpenden** - community business directory [harpenden.thewearecommunity.co.uk](https://harpenden.thewearecommunity.co.uk/).

## Loneliness - national proxy used in lieu of local data

- UK Community Life Survey 2023/24: 21% of over-75s experience loneliness "some of the time"; 5% "most of the time".
- Source: [gov.uk - Community Life Survey 2023/24](https://www.gov.uk/government/statistics/community-life-survey-202324-annual-publication/community-life-survey-202324-loneliness-and-support-networks--2).
