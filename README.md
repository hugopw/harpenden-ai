# Harpenden.AI

A vision website to pitch Harpenden Town Council on turning Harpenden into the UK's most AI-literate community - a testbed for how a town can embrace AI, safeguard its residents from job displacement, and solve real local problems together.

**Owner:** Hugo Pickford-Wardle (AI Optimist)
**Audience:** Harpenden Town Council (primary); residents and local businesses (secondary)
**Status:** Scaffold - awaiting plan approval before build.

## The hypothesis

Harpenden has an unusually high density of London-commuting knowledge workers whose roles are prime targets for AI-driven headcount reduction. The same residents have invested heavily in their children's education on the assumption of a traditional graduate-career path. If Harpenden becomes fluent in AI - as residents, parents, businesses, and a council - it can:

1. **Protect** its economic base from displacement.
2. **Equip** the next generation with skills that still matter.
3. **Solve** local systemic problems (traffic, loneliness, venue discovery, potholes, council engagement) using AI as a community tool.
4. **Demonstrate** what a modern town council can be in the AI era.

## What this repo contains

- The vision document (as a website) the council will be walked through.
- A directory of interventions (AI Night School, Sherpas AI, AI Optimist Strategy, AI Transformation Accelerator, Imaginarium, Problemarium, Chooser, plus diagnostics).
- Worked prototypes - starting with `/harpenden-traffic` - that show what community-led AI projects look like in practice.
- A council-facing version of the AI Transformation Accelerator, framed for running the council itself.
